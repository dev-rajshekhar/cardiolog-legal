CardioLog – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

CardioLog is developed and maintained by the CardioLog team. We respect your privacy and follow a “Private by Design” approach.

Data Collection and Storage

CardioLog does not collect, store, transmit, or sell health data. All health data is stored locally on your device.

No Accounts

The app does not require account creation or login. We do not have access to your identity or personal health records.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for app functionality:

  • RevenueCat – subscription management
  • PostHog / Google Crashlytics – anonymous usage and crash reporting

These services do not receive personal health data.

Data Backup and Loss

All data is stored locally on your device. If the app is uninstalled or the device is lost, data cannot be recovered.
